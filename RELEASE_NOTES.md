## Quick Start

1. Download the package for your platform
2. Extract/Install
3. Connect your floppy controller hardware
4. Launch UnifiedFloppyTool
5. Select your device from the Hardware tab
6. Insert a disk and start reading!

## Known Issues

- macOS: First launch may require Security & Privacy approval
- Linux: udev rules may need to be installed for USB device access
- Windows: Some antivirus software may flag the USB drivers
